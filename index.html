Christmas Billboard Top 100

By: Willa Liou

Welcome to my portfolio showcasing five graphs that analyze trends from the Christmas Billboard Top 100. Each visual represents unique insights drawn from the dataset. Click on the graphs to view them in detail.

Graph 1: Most Prominent Christmas Carol

This graph shows the most popular Christmas carols, ranked by the total number of weeks that each song spent on the Billboard Top 100. Jingle Bell Rock came out on top.

Graph 2: Christmas Carol Ranking

This graph displays the rankings of the top five Christmas carols from the previous graph, showing how their positions have fluctuated over time. It highlights both the consistency and changing popularity of certain songs.

Graph 3: Number of Christmas Carols in Billboard Top 100

This graph visualizes the total number of Christmas carols that made it to the Billboard Top 100 across the years, showing the trends of the prevalence of holiday music in the charts. In earlier years, there seems to be a much higher number of Christmas carols compared to today.

Graph 4: Peak Positions Histogram

This histogram illustrates the distribution of the peak positions for the Christmas carols. A lower position means a higher ranking. This helps us understand how competitive Christmas songs are over time.

Graph 5: Consecutive Topping Christmas Carols

This graph tracks the performance of Christmas carols that have topped the Billboard 100 for consecutive years. It highlights songs that have seen long-term popularity.
